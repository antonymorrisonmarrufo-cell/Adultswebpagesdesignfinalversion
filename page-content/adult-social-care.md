## Description

Find information and advice about adult social care, request support, report a concern, and discover services available to you or someone you care for in Waltham Forest.

Last updated: March 2026

---

## Hero Action Cards

- Get a care assessment
- Report abuse or neglect of an adult
- Apply for a blue badge

---

## Topics in this section

- **How adult social care works** - Find out what adult social care is, who it is for, how to get help, and what you may need to pay.
- **How to get support** - Find out how to apply for adult social care support, from making first contact through to getting a care and support plan.
- **Live independently at home** - Get equipment and assistive technology, home adaptations, falls prevention, telecare and help at home.
- **Care homes and supported living** - Find out about options if you need more support, including supported living, Shared Lives and care homes.
- **Paying for your care** - Find out how care is paid for, what you may need to contribute, and how financial assessments work, personal budgets and direct payments work or pay your care invoice.
- **Report abuse or neglect** - Report concerns about an adult at risk of abuse or neglect and find out what happens next.
- **Mental health and wellbeing** - Get mental health help, find local support for low mood or anxiety, and see how we work with the NHS.
- **Dementia, Disabilities and specialist support** - Support for adults with physical disabilities, sight or hearing loss, learning disabilities, autism, dementia and other long-term health conditions.
- **Travel and transport** - Find help with travel, including Blue Badge, Freedom Pass and community transport.

---

## Action Cards

- **Support for carers** - If you look after a family member, partner or friend, find out about carer's assessments, respite, financial help and local support.
- **Information for care professionals** - Access referral tools, professional guidance, invoicing, details, and contact information for our brokerage and commissioning teams.
