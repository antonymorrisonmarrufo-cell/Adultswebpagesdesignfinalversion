## Key Points

- for yourself
- for somebody else, with their knowledge where possible
- getting washed or dressed
- using the toilet
- getting in or out of bed
- using stairs
- moving around your home
- preparing meals
- using equipment
- home adaptations
- staying safe at home
- the person's name, address and date of birth
- contact details
- a summary of what they are finding difficult
- details of any urgent risks
- details of who is helping them now, if anyone
- We will acknowledge your request
- A member of our team will review the information
- If an assessment is needed, we will arrange to visit or speak with you
- We will agree recommendations with you
- You will receive a reference number at the end of this form
