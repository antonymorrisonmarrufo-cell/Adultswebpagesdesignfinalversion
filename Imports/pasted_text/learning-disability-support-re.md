Section:
What happens after referral?

Copy:
After you contact us:
1. We review the information you give us.
2. We decide whether the Community Learning Disability Team is the right service.
3. If needed, we carry out an assessment.
4. We talk with you about the outcomes you want and agree what support may help.
5. If you are eligible, we put a plan in place and review it regularly.

- community activities

Section:
Who should use this route

Copy:
Use this route if you are a GP, hospital team member, social worker, education professional, community worker or another professional referring an adult who may need specialist learning disability support.

Section:
What information to include

Copy:
Have the following details ready:
- name and contact details
- date of birth
- address
- why you believe the person may have a learning disability
- current risks or urgent concerns
- current support in place
- consent details where possible

Section:
What happens next

Copy:
We will review the referral and decide whether the Community Learning Disability Team is the right service.
If needed, an assessment will be arranged.
If another service is more appropriate, the referral should be redirected or signposted rather than simply rejected.

Section:
Urgent help

Copy:
For urgent social care issues outside normal working hours, call 020 8496 3000.
For mental health crisis support, call 111 and press option 2.
If there is immediate danger, call 999.

Before you start:
You will need:
- the person’s name, address and date of birth
- contact details
- a short summary of what support is needed
- details of any urgent concerns
- details of who is helping already, if anyone

What this form is for:
Use this form if you need help with:
- daily living and independence
- specialist health or support needs linked to a learning disability
- care planning or reassessment
- support to stay safe and healthy
- support to access the right service

What happens next:
We will review the information you give us.
If the Community Learning Disability Team is the right service, we will contact you about next steps.
If another service is more appropriate, we will signpost or redirect where possible.
